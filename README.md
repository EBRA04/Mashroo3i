# Mashroo3i

Mashroo3i is a web-based startup support platform designed to help entrepreneurs in Jordan transform early-stage business ideas into structured startup plans.  
It provides AI-driven idea evaluation, feasibility study generation, market analysis, financial planning, and automated business plan creation.

---

## Problem

Many entrepreneurs struggle to validate business ideas due to:

- Fragmented resources
- High cost of professional consultation
- Lack of localized market and competitor data
- Difficulty creating feasibility studies and financial projections

Mashroo3i solves this by offering an automated, centralized, and beginner-friendly platform tailored to the Jordanian market.

---

## Key Features

### User Management
- Register / Login
- Profile management
- Manage multiple startup ideas

### AI Idea Evaluation
- Idea scoring
- SWOT analysis
- Risk assessment
- Market potential estimation

### Feasibility Study Generator
- Market feasibility
- Technical feasibility
- Financial feasibility
- Exportable reports

### Market & Competitor Analysis
- Local competitor insights
- Market trends 
- Industry benchmark costs

### Financial Planning Module
- Startup cost estimation
- Operating costs estimation
- Break-even analysis
- Revenue projections

### Admin Panel 
- Manage users
- Monitor activity
- Update benchmark/reference data
